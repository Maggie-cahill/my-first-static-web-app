Seven

Seven is an infamous cocktail bar which is right in the city center. The cocktails taste amazing and the enviornment is
astouding. The light is not harsh and there's plenty of comfortable booths to sit around with your friend's on a night out!
In our opinion it is one of the nicest bars to go to with your friends.

Buskers

Busker Brownes is the main bar that everyone goes to. It is packed almost every night as it is I think almost every
students favourite bar. It has good music, good drinks, and fun company. If you want to meet lots of fun people and have an overall great night
this is the place to be.

Skeff

The Skeff in Galway City serves nice cocktails and has a great atmosphere with live music. Theres three bars with three
floors and a large smoking area. They serve decent food and are open until around 2am. Located right in Eyre Square the heart of Galway City.
Buses run until around half 11pm so if you live somewhere not directly in the city you would be able to have a nice time and still catch the last bus :)

An Púcán

An Púcán is a lovely and fancy bar right in the center of Galway City. It has an outdoor/indoor smoking area with fake grass covering the floor.
It is a bit pricey so beware but the enviornment is beautiful and definetly worth a visit!

Monroes

Monroes is a restaurant/bar, it has a good selection of food with fast and good service. They have live music as entertainment and they
also serve coffee. The drinks are good with reasonable prices, just make sure to chose seats carefully as some tables and chairs are
moved later on in the evening so that people are able to comfortably order drinks
from the bar. Open fire and comfortable enviornment definetly worth a visit!

MP Walsh

M.P Walsh is a traditional Irish Bar with a warm and welcoming atmosphere situated in the heart of Galway. Serves great
cocktails and has live music for you to enjoy, the staff is impeccable as they are helpful and friendly. Great tourist spot with a lovely
Irish whiskey selection. I personally think it is a great place to go with a couple of your buddies for a nice night. Definetly recommend going at least once!
